# Product Overview

## Product Name
Multi-Vendor Ecommerce Marketplace

## Vision
Create a marketplace where multiple sellers can list products and customers can buy from many vendors in one place.

## Problem
Many small businesses cannot build their own ecommerce store. A marketplace allows them to sell online without managing infrastructure.

## Goals
- Enable vendors to sell products online
- Allow customers to discover and purchase products
- Provide admin control over vendors and marketplace operations
